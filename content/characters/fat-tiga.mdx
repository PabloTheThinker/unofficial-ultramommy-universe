## Origin

Fat Tiga was introduced on March 4, 2025, as part of a family lore drop by @SuperSisi. Revealed as Ultra Sister's brother, Fat Tiga represents the comedic side of the Ultra Mommy Universe — a loving parody that plays on the classic Ultraman Tiga character with a humorous twist.

## Appearance

Fat Tiga takes the classic Tiga design and reimagines it with a much larger, rounder physique. The red, silver, and purple color scheme nods to both the original Tiga and the Ultra Mommy Universe's purple aesthetic.

## Powers & Abilities

- **Belly Bounce** — Can deflect attacks with his impressive midsection
- **Comedic Resilience** — Seemingly indestructible due to the power of humor
- **Family Bonding** — Draws strength from his connection to Ultra Sister
- **Dramatic Entrance** — Always arrives with maximum comedic impact

## Family Connections

Fat Tiga is canonically Ultra Sister's brother, as confirmed in the March 4, 2025 family lore drop by @SuperSisi. This makes him a unique character in the roster — the first male family member of the Ultra Mommy Universe.
