## Origin

The Ultra Sisters were revealed on March 2, 2025, in a 46-second group introduction video posted by @SuperSisi. The video showcased the full squad of giant heroines together for the first time, marking a major expansion of the Ultra Mommy Universe from a single character into a full team roster.

## Description

The Ultra Sisters represent the collective group of all giant heroines in the Ultra Mommy Universe. The group introduction video was a massive hit, pulling in 3.1 million views and 25.7k likes, confirming the franchise's viral momentum.

## Powers & Abilities

- **Combined Formation** — The team can combine their powers for devastating group attacks
- **Group Transformation** — Synchronized giant transformations
- **Coordinated Assault** — Multi-directional combat strategies
- **Team Poses** — The most dramatic group poses in tokusatsu history
