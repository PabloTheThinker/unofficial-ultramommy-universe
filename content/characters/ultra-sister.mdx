## Origin

Ultra Sister was revealed on March 3, 2025, as the newest addition to the Ultra Mommy Universe. Announced with a striking image showing her distinctive horned helmet and purple/white armor, she represents the evolution of the series into a full roster of giant heroines.

## Appearance

Ultra Sister features a dramatically different design from Ultra Mommy. Her armor is purple and white, with an imposing horned helmet that gives her a more aggressive, warrior-like silhouette. The purple/white palette has become her signature and a key part of the Ultra Mommy Universe's expanding visual identity.

## Powers & Abilities

- **Flight** — Standard Ultra-family aerial capability
- **Lightning Aura** — Surrounded by crackling energy during combat
- **Horned Helmet Strike** — A devastating charging attack using her signature headgear
- **Energy Projection** — Can channel focused beams of purple energy

## Family Connections

Ultra Sister is part of the growing Ultra family. Canon lore (established March 4, 2025) confirms that Fat Tiga is Ultra Sister's brother — a playful joke connecting the characters in family-style relationships.
