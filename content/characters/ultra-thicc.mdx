## Origin

Ultra Thicc was introduced on March 3, 2025, as part of the expanding Ultra Mommy Universe roster. A curvy variant character, Ultra Thicc leans into the series' playful, fanservice-heavy aesthetic while maintaining the tokusatsu parody tone.

## Appearance

Ultra Thicc takes the exaggerated proportions of the Ultra Mommy Universe to their logical extreme. Her design emphasizes curves and mass, making her a standout among the roster's giant heroines.

## Powers & Abilities

- **Ground Slam** — Creates shockwaves with earth-shaking impact landings
- **Thicc Shield** — An energy barrier technique unique to her fighting style
- **Gravity Manipulation** — Can increase local gravity to pin opponents
- **Power Stance** — An intimidating combat pose that boosts nearby allies
